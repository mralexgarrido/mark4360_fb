## 2024-05-23 - Micro-Feedback for Form Inputs
**Learning:** Users often don't know why their "Ad Strength" is low until they guess the hidden logic. Adding simple character count indicators (e.g., "15 / 20+ chars") directly connects user action (typing) with system feedback (Ad Strength).
**Action:** Always expose validation thresholds inline when they contribute to a "gamified" score.
